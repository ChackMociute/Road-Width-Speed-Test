Testing the preferred driving velocity of different roads

You will be presented with a number of images of different roads and must estimate at what speed you would be willing to drive on such a road. Your answer should be based entirely on the speed you feel comfortable with and not on the speed limits you may be used to. Thus you should ignore traffic signs and different speed zone regulations. Some roads are right- and some are left-hand traffic but you are requested to answer as if all roads were the handedness that you are familiar and comfortable with, i.e., to ignore which side of the road is shown.

To begin the experiment just enter your name or any identifier you like and press the "Begin" button or the enter key. You may take a break any time you like by clicking the button at the top left. To then continue the trials, simply enter the same identifier and press "Begin" again. Your response times are measured so if you do take a break, make sure to pause properly by returning to this screen.
